Dumplings of China:

Jiaozi Food Wiki

Northern China is famous for the dumplings they make. Known locally as Jiaozi, they are often made with various meat such as pork, chicken, lamb, beef, or even hearty vegetables and mushrooms. Chives, scallions, cabbage, and sour cabbage are common ingredients. Steamed or boiled they can be made in large quantities by the family and are often served on holidays or celebrations. They are symbolic of prosperity.
