Dumplings of China:

Jiaozi Food Wiki

Northern China is famous for the dumplings they make. Known locally as Jiaozi,
they are often made with various meats such as pork, chicken, lamb, beef, or even
hearty vegetables and mushrooms. Chives, scallions, cabbage, and sour cabbage
are common ingredients. Steamed or boiled they can be made in large quantities
by a family and are often served on holidays or celebrations. They are symbolic
of prosperity.
